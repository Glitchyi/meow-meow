Roses

Roses are one of the most popular flowers in the world. They are known for their beauty and are often associated with love and romance. Roses come in many different colors, each having its unique meaning.

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are tall, bright flowers known for their large yellow petals and seeds. They are often associated with happiness and positivity and are known to follow the sun's movement, a phenomenon called heliotropism.

Tulips

Tulips are spring-blooming flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors. They symbolize renewal and the arrival of spring, making them a favorite for gardens and bouquets alike.
