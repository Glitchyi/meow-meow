Lionel Messi

Forward - Inter Miami CF

Lionel Messi is one of the greatest football players of all time. Known for his extraordinary skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability, Messi has won numerous awards including multiple Ballon d'Or trophies. He spent the majority of his career at FC Barcelona, later moving to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG), and now plays for Inter Miami CF.
